Trade racer, the stock market predictor makes use of the historical data and provides a view of what investors expect for the future. Predicting how the stock market will perform is one of the most difficult things to do. There are so many factors involved in the prediction – physical factors vs. psychological, rational and irrational policies of Governments, and Global economic scenario. All these aspects combine to make share prices volatile and very difficult to predict with a high degree of accuracy.
